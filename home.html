About Materna Monitor

Materna Monitor is an all-in-one pregnancy monitoring web app and wearable technology. Intended to keep pregnant women safe and healthy, the MaternaMonitor is a smartwatch that tracks vitals like heart rate and sends them to our mobile website, which can then be shared with the obstetrician and gynecologist, The buttons on the watch help send alerts to the patients' emergency contacts along with a feature to directly call 911.

Our Hardware

Our hardware contains a heart rate monitor to track maternal heart rate. Elevated heart rate over an extended period of time can be an indicator of preeclampsia. The device also has fall detection and will call an emergency contact if the patient falls down or holds the two emergency buttons.
